Programming experience

What I have taught myself before coming in the Netherlands?

Programming has always been an exciting concept for me - my journey through programming began just like a hobby but soon evolved into a career dream. My coding path has been filled with engaging courses and hands-on experiences that have allowed me to explore various elements of web development.

My programming journey kicked off with a solid foundation in HTML. I enrolled in various online courses, including those on Khan Academy, which provided a very structured way to learn the fundamentals. Gathering more knowledge and putting it into practice, I discovered the power of markup languages in transforming ideas into digital reality.

With a solid grasp of HTML, my next logical step was to follow CSS. Understanding that the visual appeal of a website are just as important as its structure, I dived into CSS in order to be able to visualise my creativity and imgaination. The connection between HTML and CSS became evident to me as I realized how these two languages could work in harmony to bring web designs to life.

In addition to web development, my programming journey also introduced me to the world of C++. During my high school years I managed to get a brief idea of what C languages are and their usae. A dedicated lecturer provided me with a basic introduction to C++. Although I haven't dived deeper into C++ since then, the minimal exposure to C languages was enough to spark a will to learn how to build desktop applications and better my knowledge in systems programming.

My programming journey is far from over - it has just begun. I am determined to learn and explore. The knowledge I've gained in HTML and CSS has sparked my passion for creating digital solutions that make a meaningful impact. As I look ahead, I am eager to develope my programming abilities, explore new coding languages anad technologies, and embark on exciting programming adventures. With the foundational knowledge I've acquired and the passion that fuels my coding trip, I am motivated to tackle the challenges that await in my future in the vast world of programming.
