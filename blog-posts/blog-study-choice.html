Study choice

How in the world have I chosen to come in Middelburg to study ICT?

I have a made a student journey document while being a student ambassador for Unify - a small Bulgarian organization that helps students apply to university in the Netherlands. It follows the beginning of my study choice and the process of appliying.

The journey of applying to university has been a long and deliberate process, one that actually began many years ago when I was just a child. It's a path that has involved numerous steps, from discovering my interests and passions to meticulously selecting the right degree, country, and university. As I reflect on this journey, it's clear that the process of finding my niche has been a lifelong endeavor, and each decision I've made has been a significant stepping stone towards realizing my educational aspirations. Now, as I embark on the next chapter of my academic journey, I do so with a deep sense of purpose, knowing that every choice along the way has been a part of this remarkable and fulfilling adventure.
