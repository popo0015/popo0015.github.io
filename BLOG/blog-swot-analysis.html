SWOT analysis

Who am I as a person according to theSWOT analysis?

Strengths

Organisation, time management, and team leading are skills I obtained from my parents. These traits have helped me go through many obstacles and stay on top of my problems.

Opportunities

I have learned to take every opportunity I am presented with because we always learn more when we try and fail than when we do not even attempt. Being organised, reliable and authentic has helped me build trust in people around me, which is the first step towards getting closer to more possibilities for development.

Weaknesses

Although seeking perfection can be a huge positive, in my experience, it has played me several times. Both wanting to be in control of every detail and not being pleased with any result, bring unwanted stress and anxiety in my life.

Threats

Some of the obstacles that are present in my life are not necessarily in my control. I make sure to minimize anything that can be a threat to my studies. Furthermore, my parents' support is crucial because they always support me when I make mistakes.
