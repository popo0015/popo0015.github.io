Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team is a renowned force in Formula 1, known for its excellence in designing and manufacturing race cars, extensive data analysis, and cutting-edge aerodynamic research. Scuderia Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari, the iconic Italian racing team, is deeply involved in Formula 1, with a strong emphasis on crafting Formula 1 cars, engine development, and performance enhancement. For me, this is an ideal fit as my combined passion for motorsports and IT would not only fulfill my professional aspirations but also feel like a dream come true, contributing to a team I've always held in high regard.
